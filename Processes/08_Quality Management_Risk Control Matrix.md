## Abbreviations

* R: Responsible

* L: Likelihood (1-5)

* C: Consequence (1-5)

* L\*/C\*: Likelihood and Consequence after mitigation

* O: Occurrence (many times a day, daily, weekly, monthly, annually)

* ES: Effective

* EY: Efficient
